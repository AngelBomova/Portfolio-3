I started playing basketball in the Second Grade. Ever since then I fell in love with the sport. I play whenever I have free time, and in the Summer I play everyday. On top of playing, my favorite team is the Boston Celtics. When I was younger I remember watching videos on YouTube about the Celtics with Larry Bird, and they became my favorite team. Web Design.

I remember the first time I heard about web design. I was a freshman in a google meet during english, at home when my teacher invited the web design teachers to come on the meet to explain the course. It sounded interesting so I looked into it, and went to the zoom interest meeting. It was then added onto my sophomore schedule, and ever since then it became my favorite subject in school. It is great if you want to expand your creativity and express it through websites!

Exercise.

I started weight training in May sophomore year to improve myself. At first it was just to get stronger for basketball, but now it is a hobby beyond that. It helps me work hard, and stay consistent. These skills I learned from lifting weights are applicable in other areas of my life.
